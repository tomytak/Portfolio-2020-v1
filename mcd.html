Upkeeping and inspiring quality service

A collection of motivational graphics for employees of Mcdonald's to heighten the experience of customers.

Objective

Encourage McDonald's employees to bring customers a heightened expeirence through graphics inspired by the theme "the best yet."

Results

Design visually engaging and playful graphics that highlight and reflect the ideals and spirit of McDonald's through different conceptual directions.

Design System

Typography

Color Pallete

Sketches

Through exploring McDonald's graphics, I decided to highlight their individually, timelessness, and accolades through the most iconic items on their menu—burgers and fries.

Final Designs

Individuality

Timelessness

Retro-iconography is at the forefront of this graphic as it incorporates Viktor Script—inspired by old, handprinted store-signs—with the modern look of Speedee Condensed. The use of typography visually reflects on the past while appealing to today’s audiences through a familiar and bold design.

Accolades

"The best yet" calls for a history of excellence. Putting a playful spin on a metal, welcomes customers and employees to a lighthearted experience while solidifying McDonald's excellence.
