Sootheze

Sootheze is a company that manufactures plush aroma and temperature therapy products. Their products aid in alleviating physical pain and mental distress across a wide spectrum of demographics. Despite being the leader in the Microwaveable, Aroma Therapy market, updating Sootheze’s brand identity will help the product resonate with the audience. Tomy Takemura will collaborate with Sootheze in the rebranding process–resulting in a logo redesign, print collateral and a website UI.

Upon researching Sootheze's brand and reviewing their use of language, I developed their core attributes, audience, and brand personality upon which I based my designs and content.

Audience

Sootheze products fill a broad demographic need. From calming and comforting preschoolers, who love to fall asleep with the warmth and comfort provided by the product; to Seniors in assisted living facilities who cuddle our bears giving them a sense of security and relief.

brand personality

  • Welcoming We welcome those who seek comforting and holistic solutions for themselves or their loved ones.
  • Teacherly Our language is engaging and teacherly to encourage and empower people to care for themselves and others.
  • Empathetic We embrace the differences between our customers and approach them with a language that is nurturing and mindful of their experiences.
