Objective

Create imagery and choose a typeface to compose the layout for three different episodes of WNYC’s RadioLab.

Results

My designs are intended to provoke a sense of uneasiness by placing viewer in a situation that encourages them to reflect on their own definition of my chosen topics.

Layout

Morality–I designed an image to cause discomfort to make the viewer question their concept of morality.

Stress–I chose to depict the volatile nature of stress while presenting the viewer with a situation that can prolong their stress depending on how they choose to act or change their actions. The boiling pot signifies the volatile nature of stress. The situation can either go out of hand and boil over or one can turn the heat down.

The podcast discusses different methods people use to overcome mental obstacles. The viewer is put in a position of vulnerability to reflect the struggle that one undertakes to reach their goal by pulling themselves out.
