Logo Design

Honolulu Zoo

Awarded_____in the 2020 Pele Awards

A whimsical reflection of the zoo’s spirit

The Honolulu Zoo was in a need of a logo that features its founding animals with playful imagery that speaks to its interactive, family-friendly environment.

Skills
  • Concept Sketches
  • Vector Illustration
  • Typography
  • Layout
the challenge

Honor the past while embracing the present

Redesign Honolulu Zoo’s logo to appeal to families through memorable iconography that considers, responds to, and amplifies the mission and history.

the outcome

Welcome audience participation

To emphasize the zoo’s interactive nature, the logo’s puzzle-like appearance supports their mission to create meaningful experiences to foster stewardship of the world around us.

Typography
Color Study

Sketches

I sought to explore three subjects—the zoo’s royal aviary, the four animals that started the zoo—elephant, bear, monkey, and birds—and the three sections that currently divide the zoo—mammals, reptiles,  and birds.

I chose to focus on the rich history of the zoo as it solidifies its individuality. Illustrating the collection of animals that started the zoo shows its rich past, while reflecting on the zoo’s current  diverse exhibition. 

Logo Exploration

Final Design

This logo mirrors the zoo’s past while incorporating clean shapes with earthy and bright colors to modernize the imagery. I incorporated an elephant, bear, monkey, and a bird into one image–like a puzzle–to demonstrate the zoo’s interactive and family-friendly environment.
