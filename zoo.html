Honoring the past while appealing to the present

What-O-Saur is a website optimized for desktops and laptops that teaches children about the dinosaur classifications and orders through animation.

Objective

Redesign Honolulu Zoo's logo while being mindful to the mission, history, and audience.

Results

The Honolulu Zoo was originally King Kalakaua’s collection of tropical birds. The animal directors later introduced an elephant, bear, and a monkey, which became the start of the zoo. This logo reflects the zoo’s past while incorporating clean, shapes with earthy and bright colors to modernize the imagery. I incorporated these four animals into one image–much like a puzzle–to demonstrate the zoo’s interactive and family-friendly environment.
